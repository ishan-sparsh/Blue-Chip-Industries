1- New Cooling Tower

  • a. Moulded FRP Induced Draft Cooling Towers
  • b. Induced Draft Crossflow Cooling Towers
  • c. Induced Draft Counterflow Cooling Towers
  • d. Natural Draft / Fanless Cooling Towers

a. Moulded FRP Induced Draft Cooling Towers

BLUECHIP Make Moulded FRP Induced Draft Cooling Towers are specially designed for industries that prioritize affordability without compromising performance. These towers are compact and economical, making them ideal for low CAPEX industries such as small-scale manufacturing, HVAC systems, and food processing units. Constructed using seamless hand molded Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP), they offer exceptional corrosion resistance, lightweight design, and structural integrity, ensuring a long service life even in harsh environments.

Unique features include energy-efficient fans that optimize operational costs, advanced drift eliminators to reduce water loss, and easy-to-maintain modular designs. The lightweight FRP structure allows for easy transportation and quick installation. BLUECHIP ensures rigorous quality checks, guaranteeing consistent thermal performance and durability. These towers are a cost-effective cooling solution for space-constrained and budget-sensitive applications.

Moulded FRP Cooling Tower

b. Induced Draft Crossflow Cooling Towers

BLUECHIP Make Induced Draft Crossflow Cooling Towers are engineered for industries requiring high-efficiency cooling with ease of maintenance. These towers feature a crossflow design, where air flows horizontally across the falling water, ensuring uniform heat transfer with minimal pressure drop. This design makes them quieter and more energy-efficient compared to other cooling systems. The water moves down by gravity and tiny droplets are formed in the fill area.

Constructed with FRP, Steel, or Timber, they are corrosion-resistant, durable, and adaptable to various industrial environments. Key features include advanced water distribution systems for consistent performance, drift eliminators to minimize water loss, and energy-efficient fans to optimize operational costs. The open design allows easy access for cleaning and maintenance, reducing downtime. These cooling towers are ideal for industries such as power plants, chemical processing, and large HVAC systems.

Cross Flow Cooling Tower

c. Induced Draft Counterflow Cooling Towers

BLUECHIP Make Induced Draft Counterflow Cooling Towers are designed to deliver superior thermal performance with a vertical air and water flow configuration. In these towers, air moves upward against the downward flow of water, maximizing contact time and ensuring efficient heat exchange. The water is sprayed using specialized nozzles that generate conical flow / mist flow ensuring peak cooling.

Available in FRP, Steel, or Timber, these towers are built to withstand extreme conditions, offering excellent resistance to corrosion and wear. Their compact design makes them suitable for industries with limited space, such as manufacturing units, chemical plants, and HVAC systems. Unique features include energy-efficient axial fans for optimized airflow, high-performance drift eliminators to minimize water loss, and precision-engineered nozzles for uniform water distribution. BLUECHIP’s counterflow towers are known for their durability, low operational costs, and ease of installation, making them a reliable choice for long-term cooling needs.

Counter Flow Cooling Tower

d. Natural Draft / Fanless Cooling Towers

BLUECHIP Make Natural Draft / Fanless Cooling Towers are an eco-friendly cooling solution that eliminates the need for mechanical fans, relying on natural convection for airflow. These cooling towers have a typical application in Dirty Water / Ejector Systems. This design significantly reduces energy consumption, making them an environmentally sustainable choice for industrial applications such as power plants, steel mills, and petrochemical facilities.

Constructed using FRP, Timber, or Steel, these towers offer high durability, resistance to environmental stress, and minimal maintenance requirements. Key features include optimized heat transfer systems for consistent performance, lightweight yet robust structures for easy installation, and advanced drift eliminators to reduce water loss. The fanless design ensures quiet operation, zero mechanical failures, and low operating costs. BLUECHIP’s natural draft cooling towers combine sustainability, efficiency, and reliability, making them a preferred choice for industries aiming to reduce energy usage and maintenance costs.
