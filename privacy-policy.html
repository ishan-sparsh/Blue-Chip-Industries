Welcome to BLUECHIP Industries, a premier manufacturer of cooling towers based in Maharashtra, India, proudly serving both domestic and international customers. From industrial plants across India to projects worldwide, we specialize in delivering innovative thermal management solutions. At Bluechip Industries, we are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with Indian laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). This Privacy Policy provides a comprehensive overview of how we collect, use, store, disclose, and safeguard your personal and non-personal information when you visit our website, www.bluechip-industries.com (the "Site"), or engage with us as a customer, partner, or visitor. By accessing our Site or interacting with us, you agree to the practices outlined herein, as governed by Indian jurisdiction.

1. Information We Collect

As a cooling tower manufacturer with a global reach, we collect information to support our operations, enhance your experience, and fulfill your requests, all while adhering to Indian legal standards. The information we collect is categorized as follows:

a. Personal Information

Personal information (or "Personal Data" as defined under the DPDP Act) refers to data that identifies you as an individual. We collect this only when you voluntarily provide it or when it’s necessary to deliver our products and services. Examples include:

  • Contact Information: Your full name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, and physical address (e.g., within India or abroad). This may be submitted through our Site’s contact forms, quote requests for cooling towers, or inquiries about installation or support.
  • Business Transaction Details: Details such as purchase orders, billing and shipping addresses, payment information (e.g., bank account details, UPI IDs, or credit/debit card data), and GSTIN (Goods and Services Tax Identification Number) for Indian transactions or export-related documentation for international orders.
  • Account Information: If you register on our Site to access product specifications, order history, or technical resources, we may collect your username, password, and preferences (e.g., language settings).
  • Professional Correspondence: Data shared during interactions with our sales, engineering, or support teams, such as cooling tower design requirements, site conditions (e.g., temperature or humidity data), or compliance needs for Indian industrial standards (e.g., Bureau of Indian Standards).

b. Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information does not directly identify you but helps us improve our services and Site functionality. This includes:

  • Usage Data: Technical details like your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type (e.g., mobile or desktop), approximate location (derived from IP within India or abroad), pages visited (e.g., cooling tower models, technical guides), referral sources, and time spent on the Site.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tools to enhance usability, track Site performance, and personalize content (e.g., remembering your preferred cooling tower category). These are deployed in compliance with Indian IT Rules, and you can manage them via browser settings (see Section 5).
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymized statistics, such as total Site visitors or popular product inquiries, used to optimize our offerings without identifying individuals.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your information to deliver high-quality cooling towers and services, ensuring compliance with Indian laws. Specific purposes include:

  • Customer Service and Support: Responding to inquiries about cooling tower specifications, installation, or maintenance; providing technical assistance; and fulfilling requests for quotes, product documentation, or site visits (e.g., within India or for export).
  • Order Fulfillment: Processing orders for cooling towers, spare parts, or services; managing shipping logistics (domestic or international); and issuing invoices or payment confirmations, including GST-compliant documentation for Indian customers.
  • Product Improvement: Analyzing feedback and usage patterns to enhance our cooling tower designs, adapt to Indian climatic conditions, and develop new features or accessories.
  • Marketing and Communications: Sending updates about our latest cooling tower models, industry developments, or promotional offers (e.g., discounts for Indian industrial buyers or export incentives). You may opt out at any time (see Section 5).
  • Website Optimization: Improving the Site’s design, content, and functionality based on user interactions, ensuring accessibility for our Indian and international audience.
  • Legal Compliance: Adhering to Indian laws, such as maintaining records under the Companies Act, 2013 for taxation or the DPDP Act for data protection, and responding to lawful requests from Indian authorities.
  • Security: Protecting our Site and business from cyber threats, fraud, or unauthorized access, as required under the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures) Rules, 2011.

3. How We Share Your Information

BLUECHIP Industries does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your data only as permitted under Indian law:

  • Service Providers: We engage trusted vendors to support our operations, such as:
    • Website hosting and IT service providers (e.g., Indian or global cloud platforms).
    • Logistics companies (e.g., Indian couriers or international shippers like Blue Dart or DHL) for delivering cooling towers.
    • Payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay, Paytm) to process transactions securely. These entities are classified as "Data Processors" under the DPDP Act and are contractually bound to protect your information per Indian standards.
  • Business Partners: Limited data (e.g., shipping details) may be shared with distributors or installation contractors (in India or abroad) to fulfill your order, with your consent or as part of a transaction.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required by Indian courts, government agencies (e.g., GST authorities, cybercrime units), or under laws like the Information Technology Act, 2000 or DPDP Act.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred to a successor entity, with assurances of compliance with Indian data protection laws.
  • Safety and Rights Protection: We may share data to safeguard our employees, customers, or business interests (e.g., resolving disputes), as permitted under Indian law.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information, aligning with the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures) Rules, 2011 and DPDP Act requirements:

  • Technical Safeguards: Encryption for data transmission (e.g., SSL/TLS on our Site), secure servers with firewalls, and access controls to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Physical Safeguards: Restricted entry to our facilities at G88 Tarapur MIDC, where physical records (e.g., order documents) are stored in locked cabinets or secure areas.
  • Organizational Safeguards: Employee training on Indian data protection laws, confidentiality agreements, and internal audits to ensure compliance. While we strive to maintain high security, no system is entirely risk-free. In case of a data breach, we will notify you and the Data Protection Authority of India (once established) as mandated by the DPDP Act.

5. Your Choices and Rights

Under Indian law, particularly the DPDP Act, you have control over your data:

  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional emails or SMS by clicking the "unsubscribe" link or emailing office@bluechip-industries.com with your request.
  • Cookies and Tracking: Adjust your browser settings to accept, reject, or delete cookies. Disabling them may affect Site features like saved preferences.
  • Data Rights (per DPDP Act):
    • Right to Access: Request confirmation of whether we process your data and obtain a summary of it.
    • Right to Correction: Ask us to rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
    • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations (e.g., tax records).
    • Right to Nominate: : Designate a nominee to manage your data in case of incapacity or death.
    • Right to Withdraw Consent:Revoke consent for data processing where it was the basis for collection (e.g., marketing). To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at office@bluechip-industries.com with your request and identity proof. We will respond within 30 days, as required by the DPDP Act.

6. Third-Party Links

Our Site may include links to external websites, such as industry partners or suppliers. These sites are governed by their own privacy policies, and Bluechip Industries is not liable for their practices under Indian law. We advise reviewing their policies before sharing data.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our Site and services are intended for industrial and commercial clients, not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 (the age of majority in India). If such data is collected inadvertently, we will delete it promptly upon notification at office@bluechip-industries.com

8. International Data Transfers

While we serve an international customer base, we are governed by Indian jurisdiction. Your data is primarily stored and processed in India. If transferred abroad (e.g., to a cloud server or logistics partner), we ensure:

  • Compliance with Section 16 of the DPDP Act, which may restrict transfers to certain countries based on government notifications (once issued).
  • Contractual safeguards with recipients to maintain Indian-level protection. Such transfers occur only for order fulfillment (e.g., shipping cooling towers overseas) or with your consent.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data as per Indian legal requirements:

  • Transactional Data: Up to 7 years under the Companies Act, 2013 and GST laws for tax and audit purposes.
  • Marketing Data: Until you opt out or it becomes obsolete.
  • Usage Data: Typically 12-24 months for analytics, unless required longer by law. You may request erasure earlier, subject to these obligations, by emailing office@bluechip-industries.com

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or Indian laws. Updates will be posted here with a revised "Effective Date." Significant changes will be notified via email or a Site banner, as required under the DPDP Act.
