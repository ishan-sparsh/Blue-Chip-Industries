SPARES

BLUECHIP MAKE Spares are designed to ensure seamless cooling tower operations with a comprehensive range of high-quality components, including fill media, fans, motors, drift eliminators, nozzles, and water distribution systems. Engineered for compatibility, reliability, and efficiency, BLUECHIP-approved spares minimize downtime and enhance performance, making them an essential choice for effective maintenance and extended service life. Trust BLUECHIP MAKE Spares to deliver unmatched quality and durability for your cooling tower needs.

I) Mechanical Equipment

1.1 BLUECHIP Aero foil-Type Fans:

  • FRP/FRVE Blades with HDG Hub: Lightweight, energy-efficient, and available in multiple configurations.
  • Aluminum Blades with Hub: Durable, low power consumption, and long-lasting.
  • Plastic Blades with Aluminum Hub: Ideal for small cooling towers.
  • Extended Shaft Flange Mounted (New India/Hindustan) for Direct Drive Application: Belt-free design, IE2, IE3, or IE4 efficiency ratings.
  • Foot Mounted for Geared Drive Application (BBL/CGL/ABB/Siemens): Suitable for large cooling towers, universally accepted.
  • BLUECHIP make Spiral Bevel Right Angle Gearboxes: Factor >=2.5% efficiency >=98% with robust design, minimal maintenance.
  • Materials: HDG Steel, SS304, Carbon Fiber, SS316, Hastelloy Dynamically balanced for superior durability.
  • BLUECHIP Fan Guards: Safety-compliant, durable, and corrosion-resistant making operations inherently safe.
  • Fan Cylinders: Optimize airflow, reduce turbulence, and enhance cooling efficiency.
  • Fan Rings: Suitable for small fans and direct drive application leading to maintenance free systems.

II) Fills, Drift Eliminators, and Fill Supports

  • BLUECHIP PVC & ABS Honeycomb Fills: Compact design for clean and high-temperature applications.
  • Splash and Trickle Fills: Perfect for dirty water and high TSS/TDS applications.
  • BLUECHIP Designs: S-Type, Wave-Type, Herringbone, and Honeycomb for minimized water loss.
  • Materials: HDG Steel, Pultruded FRP, GRP, and SS304 for durability and corrosion resistance.

III) Water Distribution Systems

  • Available in PVC with FRP coating, HDG Steel, and SS304.
  • PP / ABS precision-engineered for efficient water distribution.
  • For Crossflow cooling towers to regulate flow for optimized performance

IV) Casing, Louvers, and Partitions

  • Durable FRP and ACB options.
  • Optimized airflow, reduced water splash, and structural stability.

V) Cooling Tower Structures

  • Treated Timber, Pultruded FRP, and Moulded FRP for resilience in demanding environments. All types of cooling towers can be designed with the given materials.

VI) Instrumentation

  • BLUECHIP flameproof solutions for safety and reliability.
